Government Schemes & Benefits for Farmers

Empowering Farmers, Enriching Society

Introduction

India’s farmers are supported by a range of government schemes that provide financial aid, insurance, credit, and modern technology. These initiatives aim to improve livelihoods and ensure sustainable agriculture.

Major Government Schemes

  • PM-KISAN: ₹6,000/year direct income support to small and marginal farmers.
  • PM Fasal Bima Yojana: Crop insurance with low premiums (2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi).
  • Kisan Credit Card (KCC): Easy access to credit for seeds, fertilizers, and equipment.
  • Digital Farmer ID: Unique IDs linked to land records for streamlined benefits.
  • Horticulture Subsidy: ₹15,000–₹1,40,000/acre for fruits, vegetables, flowers, and spices.

Benefits to Farmers

Financial Security
Direct cash transfers and subsidized loans.

Risk Management
Insurance against crop loss and disasters.

Modernization
Access to new tools and digital records.

Inclusive Growth
Support for SC/ST and women farmers.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply through local agricultural offices, Common Service Centres (CSCs), or online portals.
